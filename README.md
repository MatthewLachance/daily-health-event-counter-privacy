# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 20, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Daily Health Event Counter app ("the App") handles information.

The App is designed to respect your privacy. It does not collect, store, or share personal or sensitive information.

---

## Information Collection

The App does **not collect any personal information**.

This includes, but is not limited to:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Health data stored outside the device

All data entered into the App remains solely on your device.

---

## Use of Data

Any data you enter into the App is stored **locally on your device only**.

The App does not transmit, upload, or process your data on external servers.

---

## Data Sharing

The App does **not share any data** with third parties.

---

## Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms.

No data is stored in the cloud or on external servers.

You are responsible for securing access to your device.

---

## File Import and Export

The App allows you to **import and export data files** only when you explicitly choose to do so.

- File access is **entirely user-initiated**
- The App does not access files or storage automatically
- You select the location and files involved

Any exported data is stored wherever you choose and is subject to the privacy and security of that location.

---

## Permissions

The App does not require any device permissions for normal operation.

If file access is needed (for import/export), it is performed through system-provided file selection tools under your control.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
